# 8. Proof Families

Initial proof families:

- `regionMembershipProof`
- `qualityThresholdProof`
- `consentPurposeScopeProof`
- `freshnessProof`
- `notRevokedProof`
- `anonymousRateLimitProof`
- `deliveryZoneEligibilityProof`
- `postalEquivalentAreaProof`

Each family must define private inputs, public signals, accepted roots, failure
states, and replay behavior.
